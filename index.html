ABOUT US

We are a developer duo from Hungary, with different skills. We truly believe in community support and open-source.


We are creating solutions mainly for Minecraft and related services, but we don't get scared if we need to develop in a totally different area.

Both of us started coding for Minecraft at a young age, and grew up learning along the way.


Interestingly, all of our knowledge was self-taught, we didn't learn programming in school.

Fun fact: we've only met each other 3 times in real life, as we live in totally different parts of our country.


Despite this challenge, we've become best friends, won some competitions and created services/products that gained big support by the community.

OUR PROJECTS

Icicle

Icicle is one of our main projects.
It is a Spring inspired framework, design for Minecraft development. It provides a complete bean, command, GUI, protocol system & so on.

FrostedEngineering

FrostedEngineering is our second main project.
It's a plugin framework aiming to provide mod-like experience with just resourcepacks and plugins, no client side hassle with mods and libs.

NBTLib

NBTLib is an implementation of Notch's NBT format in Java.
It has some useful helper methods along with an easy-to-use design.

Resource Host

ResourceHost is a free and easy-to-use temporary resourcepack hosting service, made with Spring. It's a spinoff/side project of FrostedEngineering.

FrostedBot

This was one of our favourite projects to work on.
It is a very advanced music bot, with features (took us literally weeks to implement 1 feature) no other bots has implemented. Sadly, due to YouTube's sudden war against such bots, it has never seen the light of the day.
